Emily Franke

Acting

Emily is a graduate of Illinois State University with a B.S. in Acting. She is excited to be starting her post graduate acting career in Illinois State University’s 2021 Acting Showcase. At Illinois State University, Emily played Sarah Brown in Guys and Dolls (Irene Ryan Nomination), Shioban in The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Nighttime, Medium Allison in Fun Home and Amy in Green River. She was also cast in and composed music for ISU’s production of “Twelfth Night”. Emily will be based in Chicago. Resume and Headshot are attatched below.
